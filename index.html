About us

This battery of logic design related solvers and converters was implemented by a group of undergraduate students of the Computer Science Dept of the University of Thessaly, as a by-product of an unofficial summer school aiming at the development and enrichmnent of their Python programming skills. The creation of the application at hand aims to help fellow students to learn and understand basic logic design concepts while having a versatile helper at their disposal.
